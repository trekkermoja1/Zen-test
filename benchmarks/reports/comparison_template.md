## Appendix C: Methodology

### Scoring System
- **Detection Rate**: Percentage of expected vulnerabilities found (weight: 30%)
- **Precision**: Ratio of true positives to total findings (weight: 20%)
- **Recall**: Ratio of true positives to expected findings (weight: 15%)
- **F1 Score**: Harmonic mean of precision and recall (weight: 15%)
- **Speed**: Based on 1/duration (weight: 10%)
- **Cost Efficiency**: Findings per dollar (weight: 10%)
