## 📱 Social Media Guidelines

### Sharing About Zen AI Pentest

When posting about Zen AI Pentest:

✅ **DO:**
- Share your experience and projects
- Tag us: @ZenAIPentest
- Use hashtags: #ZenAIPentest #AIPentesting #Cybersecurity
- Give constructive feedback
- Share educational content

❌ **DON'T:**
- Share confidential information
- Post exploit details without responsible disclosure
- Spam or unsolicited promotion
- Share false information
